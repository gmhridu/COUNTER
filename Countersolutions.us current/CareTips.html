Countertop Care & Maintenance Tips

Marble Care

  • Clean spills immediately with a soft cloth
  • Use pH-neutral cleaners only
  • Seal every 6-12 months
  • Avoid acidic cleaners and vinegar

Granite Care

  • Use warm water and mild dish soap
  • Seal annually for best protection
  • Use cutting boards to prevent scratches
  • Avoid harsh chemicals and abrasive cleaners

Quartz Care

  • No sealing required
  • Use mild soap and water for cleaning
  • Stain and heat resistant
  • Avoid direct heat and harsh chemicals

General Care Guidelines

Daily Maintenance

  • Wipe spills immediately with a soft cloth
  • Use coasters under glasses and cups
  • Clean with mild soap and warm water

Preventive Care

  • Use trivets for hot items
  • Use cutting boards for food preparation
  • Regularly inspect for damage or wear
