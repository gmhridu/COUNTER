Our Story

Founded in 2008, Counter Solutions has been serving the Burlington area with exceptional countertop services for over 15 years. Our journey began with a simple mission: to provide high-quality countertops at affordable prices while maintaining the highest standards of craftsmanship.

What started as a small family business has grown into a trusted name in countertop installation and design. Our commitment to excellence and customer satisfaction has earned us a reputation as one of the leading countertop providers in the region.
